Our Story

Located on the historic West San Antonio Street, The Water Stop brings together the best of West Texas hospitality with American comfort food classics.

We believe in good food, good drinks, and good company. Our covered patio is the perfect spot to watch the Marfa sunset.

We're proud to serve locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible and support our fellow Marfa businesses. Everything is made with care and attention to detail.
